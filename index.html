What is IEEE?

  • IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to fostering technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.
  • Branches give students a peer community and connections to faculty and industry, enabling accomplishments beyond the classroom through events and projects.
  • Branches host technical meetings, interest groups, competitions, and outreach programs in coordination with local sections and societies.

Why Join?

  • Get exclusive access to opportunities, funding, and a global professional network that supports your growth from student life to early career.
  • IEEE offers free hosting and tools that help student branches run impactful programs efficiently.
  • Network locally and globally with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.
  • Gain funding for events and develop leadership skills by organizing initiatives within IEEE.
