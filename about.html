      About

      The IEEE Student Branch at JSSATEB connects students with a vibrant professional ecosystem to learn, build, and lead through technical events, mentoring, and community service aligned with the IEEE mission of advancing technology for humanity.

      The Branch bridges academic learning with real-world competencies via projects, research exposure, and collaborations with sections, societies, and affinity groups.

      • Activities include technical talks, workshops, special projects, outreach, and participation in regional and global IEEE programs.
      • Members gain exposure to professional practices, leadership development, and pathways to internships and research through IEEE networks.

      What we do

      The Branch hosts year‑round activities that help members explore new domains, build portfolio‑ready work, and expand professional networks. Events are designed with hands‑on learning, mentorship, and measurable outcomes in mind.

      • Workshops and bootcamps in AI/ML, embedded systems, cloud, cybersecurity, and software engineering.
      • Technical talks and panels featuring researchers, industry engineers, and IEEE speakers. Hackathons, design challenges, and coding contests including IEEEXtreme participation.
      • Community outreach via STEM sessions, Tech4Good, and EPICS in IEEE initiatives.
